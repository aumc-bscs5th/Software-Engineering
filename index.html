Welcome to Software Engineering Course offered at Air University Multan Campus for undergraduate Classes.

Course Code - SE-225 Credit Hours: 3-1 --> 04 Lab Yes Pre-requiste Object Oriented Programming Weekly Classes 03 hours Labs - 03 hours This Course is Prerequisite For: SE327,SE329 ### The objectives of this course are: -- To introduce the students to the principles and techniques of Software Engineering leading to better analysis and design -- To enable students to get familiar themselves with state of the Art Software Engineering Construction Activities. -- To get students equipped with Software Engineering Analysis, Design and Testing Techniques. -- To introduce them to various structured modeling diagrams -- To familiarize them with writing various Software Engineering documents

Grading and Generel Course Polices

  • Visit class folder regularly for course updates, announcements, and for other course materials. 
  • Assignments and/or grade percentages are subject to change. The breakdown is as follows:
  • No makeup tests will be given
  • No late assignments will be accepted.
  • All individual assignments submitted should be the outcome of individual work only.
  • Plagiarized work shall lead to an “F”.
  • There should be clear division of work in the group projects.
  • Failing to meet the deliverables dead line will lead to “F” in that deliverable.
